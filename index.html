Audience Insights: Who Responds to What?

Effective creative is not one-size-fits-all. Different demographics respond to different psychological triggers and messaging styles. Use the filters below to explore how creative strategy should adapt for generational and gender-specific audiences.

Gen Z & Millennials

  • Value authenticity; prefer user-generated content (UGC) over polished ads.
  • Seek diverse, relatable representation.
  • Turn to social media for financial advice, making social proof critical.
  • Respond to educational content and expert insights.

Gen X & Baby Boomers

  • Value authority and expertise; prefer professional advisors in ads.
  • Respond to credentials, tenure, and institutional affiliations.
  • Show more risk-averse behavior, requiring conservative messaging.
  • Prefer detailed information in traditional formats.

Engaging Female Investors

With women controlling 40% of global wealth, tailored creative is essential.

  • Prefer empathy-driven messaging and female advisors.
  • Respond better to informative, illustrated content over aggressive sales tactics.
  • Seek reassurance and conservative approaches.
  • Value seeing women represented in authoritative financial roles.
